What is KISST?

Little Mobile Conversations

Automating customer contact doesn't have to be expensive or difficult, or require weeks of development. KISST services are little messaging conversations that connect people and programs quickly and easily.

Simple and Standardized

KISST has a small set of conversations to pick from, such as reporting events, taking surveys and signing up for service. You don't need a geek to customize your prompts, or to change type of the conversation.

Connects to Your Business Data

Data collected from KISST conversations is downloadble from our portal, and is emailed to your inbox. And, the transcript and structured data is can be quickly attached to most major cloud services such as Google Docs, Salesforce and Zendesk, or your own custom backend.

Attaches to Normal Phone Numbers

Each KISST service attaches to a telephone number or to social messaging, and listens and responds on behalf of your business. There are no clients to install, and works even when only text messsaging does.
