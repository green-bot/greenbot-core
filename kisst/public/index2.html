What is KISST?

Automated Customer Conversations

KISST automates customer conversations using messaging. Each KISST service attaches to a telephone number, and listens and responds on behalf of your business. When the conversation is over, KISST will email you a transcript of the conversation. KISST allows you to run your business, while it answers your customers in real time.

KISST services can be deployed in minutes, and can be attached to a new number, or it can service your existing land line.
